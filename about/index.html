About Us

Humanities Immersed Learning is a small team building history and civics games that students actually want to play.

We believe students learn history by living it, not memorizing it. Every game we build applies Experiential Narrative Education, our framework for turning academic content into roleplay, choice, and consequence, so learning actually sticks.
