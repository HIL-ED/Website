⚔ MEDIEVAL FATE ⚔

A Middle Ages Survival RPG
📜 THE YEAR IS 1347 AD
Welcome, traveler. You are about to begin your life in Medieval Europe.

The world is harsh. Disease, famine, and war threaten all, from the lowest serf to the mightiest noble.

Your fate is determined by the dice of fortune. Will you survive?

✦ Discover your place in the world and begin your journey ✦
📖 HOW TO PLAY
► Reveal your character's place in Medieval society: your birth determines your fate.
► Each year, a new event will challenge your character's survival.
► Fate decides your outcome: Success, Partial Success, or Failure. Your station in life and circumstances affect your chances.
► Three hardships = death. Run out of money = death within two years.
► The Teacher is always the King who owns the land.
