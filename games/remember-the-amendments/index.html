How to Play

  • Round 1 — Definitions Match each amendment number to what it says and protects
  • Round 2 — Structural Mechanics Match amendments to how they changed the executive, legislative, or judicial branch
  • Round 3 — Societal Impact Match amendments to their real-world effects on American society

12 randomly chosen amendments · 24 cards per round · timed trials
All three round times add up — lower is better. Compete with classmates!
